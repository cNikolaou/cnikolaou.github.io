About

Interested in using technology for positive social impact.

Most recently, I have been working on computer network security. I have been experimenting with link-flood and analysing their behaviour and impact, with the goal of designing new defense mechanisms. In this project I have been working with Gianni Anticchi and Stefano Vissicchio. In the past I was also working with Andrew Moore.
