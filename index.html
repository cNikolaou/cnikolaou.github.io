About

I am Christos Nikolaou and I am a research assistant at the University of Cambridge. I am generally interested on antyhing related to computer systems and machine learning. Currently I am a member of the Networks and Operating Systems group where I am working with Andrew Moore and Gianni Anticchi and we are also collaborating with Stefano Vissicchio.

Most recently, I have been working on computer networks and security, focusing on distributed denial-of-service (DDoS) attacks, their impact on different real-world network topologies, and on differnt way that we can mitigate the attacks.
